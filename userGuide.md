# Suburbmates User Guide

**Website:** Suburbmates - Melbourne Hyper-Local Marketplace & Business Directory

**Purpose:** Discover and connect with verified local businesses in your Melbourne suburb, supporting your community while enjoying seamless shopping experiences.

**Access:** Public access for browsing; login required for creating businesses and managing profiles.

---

## Powered by Manus

Suburbmates is built with cutting-edge technology and modern architecture:

**Frontend:** React 19 + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS 4 + shadcn/ui components for responsive, accessible interfaces

**Backend:** Express 4 + tRPC 11 providing type-safe API contracts with zero runtime errors

**Database:** MySQL with Drizzle ORM for reliable data persistence and migrations

**Authentication:** Manus OAuth with passwordless email verification for secure, frictionless login

**Deployment:** Auto-scaling infrastructure with global CDN ensuring 99.9% uptime and lightning-fast performance worldwide

## Using Your Website

### Browse the Directory

Click "Browse Directory" from the homepage to explore verified local businesses. Use the sidebar filters to narrow your search by suburb or business name. Each business card displays key information including verification status, location, and services offered. Click "View Profile" to see complete details, contact information, and opening hours.

### Create Your Business Profile

Sign in with your email, then navigate to "Create Your Business" from your dashboard. Fill in your business details including name, ABN, location, contact information, and description. Your business will appear in the directory once created, helping local customers discover you.

### Manage Your Account

Visit your dashboard to view all your businesses, update your profile information, and manage your preferences. Your dashboard shows your business listings, profile settings, and account activity in one convenient location.

### Search and Filter

Use the advanced search functionality to find businesses by name or suburb. The directory automatically displays only active, verified businesses. Filtering by suburb helps you discover local options in your area.

## Managing Your Website

Access the **Management UI** (right panel) to control your platform:

- **Preview Panel:** See live changes as you build
- **Code Panel:** Download all project files
- **Dashboard:** Monitor website analytics and performance metrics
- **Database Panel:** View and manage all business and user data
- **Settings Panel:** Configure website title, logo, domains, and secrets
- **Publish Button:** Deploy your website to production (requires checkpoint)
