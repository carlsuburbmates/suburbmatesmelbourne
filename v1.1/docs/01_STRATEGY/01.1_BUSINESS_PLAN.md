## Executive Summary

**Suburbmates** is a hyper-local digital marketplace solving the fragmented, untrustworthy nature of existing platforms (Facebook Marketplace, Gumtree, local Facebook groups). We connect service providers (freelancers, digital product creators, small service businesses) with nearby customers in their Local Government Area (LGA), enabling fair, transparent, secure transactions with automation-first vendor support.

**Core Value Proposition:**
- **For Vendors:** Low barrier to entry (3-min signup), fair commission (6-8% vs. Gumtree 13%+), chatbot-driven support, vendor vetting (ABN verification [05] §1.3)
- **For Customers:** Curated local vendors, secure payments (Stripe) [10], transparent commission breakdown [10] §5, confidence through vetted vendors and automation-first support
- **For Platform:** 6-8% commission per transaction, $20/month featured slot fees, geographic expansion path

**Phase 1 MVP Focus:** Melbourne 12 LGAs, core marketplace features, 44+ AI automations [172], chatbot-heavy support model, vendor suspension/appeals [05] §5, queue algorithm [03] §4, rate limiting [03] §5, commission transparency [10]

**Phase 1 Gap Remediation (Nov 9, 2025):** All 6 critical gaps fully addressed: support automation, policy enforcement [05/07/172], commission transparency [10/172], queue algorithm [03/172], rate limiting [03/08/10/172]

## Support Model (MVP, No SLA Commitments)

- **FAQ Chatbot (Claude API, ~$2/month):** Answers common questions from indexed docs.
- **Billing auto-response (Stripe API):** On billing queries, bot fetches recent charges, invoices, and provides next steps.
- **Technical auto-response (Sentry):** Bot surfaces recent errors/logs for self-help.
- **Instant resolution rate:** Target **~70%** of tickets resolved by chatbot.
- **Founder load:** **<1 hr/week** for exceptions and product-critical issues.
- **Legal stance:** **No SLA offered** in MVP. Reliability is prioritized; commitments may be revisited post–market validation.

### Market Alignment
- Market comparables (Gumtree/Facebook/eBay) **do not offer SLAs**.
- MVP focus: **prove reliability**, not promises. Reassess potential SLAs in **Month 4–6** if demand is clear.
